    • Resume and Work History

  • Disney Consumer Products

    Managing Disney brands, products, and partners was definitely a career highlight. I led product development for all of Disney's brands under the consumer electronics umbrella. The Consumer Electronics business manufactured 1000’s of products a year generating over 80 million dollars in revenue.

  • Pixar Brand Development

    Pixar is special to me. I worked on the Pixar brands at Mattel and Disney. We would screen the movie very early and use that information to build out the product lines. Some of my favorite product launches were Cars 2, Toy Story 3, Inside Out, and Big Hero 6.

  • Disney Infinity Products

    Disney Infinity was on Bob Iger's top 5 priority list for all of Disney. I worked with the Infinity core development team, PDP, and Avalanche Games to bring the line, and the toys to life. The products made over 1 billion in revenue!

  • Mattel

    Mattel is my foundation of corporate design. We moved as a global unit. I contributed brainstorming ideas, sketches, and design presentations that advanced products to market.

  • Radica

    My love of electronics started at Radica. We reviewed items that would eventually become Alexa, MindFlex, and Google Glass. I constructed UX/UI documents, wrote code specs, and tested the software on physical hardware. Radica was the best!

  • Fisher Price

    Fisher Price was a great internship and my first step into toy design. The Baby Gymnastics Soccer Goal was designed and approved for production in the short summer that I had with the company.

  • Creativity Studio

    Creativity Studio was the number 1 entertainment application for iPad. Learn step by step how to draw Disney characters from real Disney artists. Disney, SDI, and Artamata brought the product to life.

  • Puppy Tweets

    Made at Mattel. Puppy Tweets gives your dog a voice on Twitter. I led product development from the programs UX/UI, hardware design, choosing the sensors, to architecting the speech and motion patterns.

  • Tetris 360

    Tetris 360 was an inventor idea from IDEO. Radica optioned the idea and I got to work! Hundreds of builds were tested and reworked. The product was a success and I followed up with Uno 360 and Pictionary 360.

  • PhotoCo

    At PhotoCo, I managed the top selling product line called the PhotoShare. The Photoshare is a digital Photo Album and Frame that sold at Wal-mart and other retailers.

  • Moen Design

    Moen was a great opportunity to develop consumer products. My role focused on hardware. Moen has a great creative culture!

  • Frame-O-Dex

    Ideated, designed, sourced, funded, manufactured, shipped, distributed, and patented by my LLC Global Urban Market.
