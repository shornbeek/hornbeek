Disney Consumer Products

Managed Disney brands, products, UX and UI. I led product development for all of Disney's brands under the consumer electronics umbrella. The Consumer Electronics business generated over 80 million dollars in revenue.

Pixar Brand Development

Pixar is special to me. I worked on the Pixar brands at Mattel and Disney. We would screen the movie very early and use that information to build out the product lines. Some of my favorite product launches were Cars 2, Toy Story 3, Inside Out, and Good Dinosaur.

Disney Infinity Products

Disney Infinity was on Bob Iger's top 5 priority list for all of Disney. I worked with the Infinity core development team, PDP, and Avalanche Games to bring the line, and the toys to life. The products made over 1 billion in revenue!

Mattel

Mattel is my foundation of corporate design. We moved as a global unit. I contributed brainstorming ideas, sketches, and design presentations that advanced products to market.

Radica

My love of electronics started at Radica. We reviewed items that would eventually become Alexa, MindFlex, and Google Glass. I constructed UX/UI documents, wrote code specs, and tested the software on physical hardware. Radica was the best!

Fisher Price

Fisher Price was a great internship and my first step into toy design. The Baby Gymnastics Soccer Goal was designed and approved for production in the short summer that I had with the company.
