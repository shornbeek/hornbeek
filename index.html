Software Work Samples

  • Twitterbot: 2018
    • The Twitter bot retweets tweets with specific tags to @School_Today_
    • Built with the NPM package Twit , Nodejs and deployed to Heroku as a worker
    • Twitter API keys and OAuth were created and implemented
    • The bot uses key words and gathers tweet id's. Every 15 mins the bot selects an id and retweets the selected tweet
    • Twitter following section links to all gov officals twitter profiles.
    • Daniel Shiffman’s Coding Train Youtube videos were referenced when building this project
  • Stop School Shootings Today: 2018
    • Stop School Shootings today is like the Drudge Report for gun control
    • Built with many RSS feeds from top news organizations Huffington Post , Fox News , CNN
    • Feed2JS was used to add RSS feeds in the index.html webpage
    • Twitrss.me was used to get a twitter "live" search as RSS feeds
    • TheTwitrss.me files were then passed into Feed2JS and the final code was implemented into the index.html file.
    • A HTML hard coded table of contacts and schools were added as seporate pages.

    • Resume and Work

  • Disney

    Managing Disney brands and products was definitely a career highlight. I lead product development for all of Disney's brands under the consumer electronics umbrella. The Consumer Electronics business manufactured 1000’s of products a year generating over 80 million dollar in revenue.

  • Pixar

    Pixar is special to me. I worked on the Pixar brands at Mattel and Disney. We would screen the movie very early and use that information to build out the product lines with our partner companies. Some of my favorite product launches were Inside Out and Big Hero 6.

  • Infinity

    Disney Infinity was on Bob Iger's top 5 priority list for all of Disney. I worked with the Infinity core development team, PDP, and Avalanche Games to bring the line to life. I assisted in the development figures and starter kit. I lead the development for the graphics for the accessories for all major markets. The products made over 1 billion in revenue.

  • Mattel

    Mattel is my foundation of corporate design. We moved as a global unit. I contributed brainstorming ideas, sketches, and design presentations that got products to market.

  • Radica

    My love of production level electronics started at Radica. We got to review items that would eventually become Alexa, MindFLex, and Google Glass. I constructed UX/UI documents, wrote code specs, and tested the software on physical hardware. Radica was the best!

  • Fisher Price

    Fisher Price was a great internship and my first step into toy design. The Baby Gymtastics Soccer Goal was designed and approved for production in the short time I had with the company.
